Tribute to Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla, a visionary inventor and electrical engineer, is best known for his contributions to the development of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system. Born on July 10, 1856, in Smiljan (now part of Croatia), Tesla's innovations and ideas have left a lasting impact on the world.

Key Achievements

  • Development of AC electricity supply system.
  • Invention of the induction motor and transformer.
  • Wireless transmission of energy.
  • Contributions to the development of X-rays and radio waves.
  • Over 300 patents filed worldwide.

Legacy

Tesla's ideas and inventions have had a profound impact on the world of science and technology. His work laid the foundation for many modern conveniences and technologies that we often take for granted today. Despite facing various challenges during his lifetime, Tesla's legacy continues to inspire scientists, engineers, and inventors around the globe.
